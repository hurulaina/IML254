C:M3

'C' means comic here and the 'M3' stands for the three popular comics which are Manhwa, Manhua and Manga. What's the difference or what are they you ask? Well for starters, Manhwas are comic adaptaions of korean novels. Similarly, Manhuas are comic adaptations of chinese novels and mangas are adaptations of japanese novels.
